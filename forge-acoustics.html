Acoustic Engineering

DRIVER

PROCESS

How a driver becomes a reference.

Not a sequence of steps so much as a set of standards every unit has to clear.

Measure

Every prototype is measured in an anechoic chamber before anyone is allowed to listen with their ears — the graph has to earn the audition.

Listen

A rotating panel of mastering and mixing engineers blind-tests each revision against the current reference model, not against competitors.

Wear

Comfort prototypes go home with engineers for two-week trials — fatigue at hour eight matters more than first impression at minute one.

Fail on purpose

Every housing is drop-tested, sweat-tested, and cable-yanked well past spec before a unit is cleared for a serial number.
